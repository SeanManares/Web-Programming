Recipe: Fruit Salad

This classic fruit salad consists of various kinds of fruits, served as a cold dessert that is perfect for any occasions. Family and friends will surely love it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can fruit cocktail in light syrup
  • 1 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 bottle nata de coco
  • 1 bottle coconut strings
  • 1/4 cup cheese

Method:

  1. Using a strainer, drain the fruit cocktail. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine heavy whipping cream and condensed milk.
  3. Using a hand mixer, whisk until soft peaks form.
  4. Add the fruit cocktail. Mix.
  5. Add nata de coco(coconut gel) and coconut strings..
  6. Optional: Mix in the cut cheese.
  7. Refrigerate or chill atleast four hour. Serve cold.
